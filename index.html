Italy

Italy has spectacular cities, classical ruins, mind blowing museums, beautiful mountains, beaches and of cources the fantastic Italian food. Some places to visit include, Rome, Venice, Florence, Milan, Naples, Amalfi and Cinque Terre

Maldives

Maldives is located in the Indian Ocean making it a tropical place. Maldives can be describes as a 'getaway' were you can go and have a relaxing stay combines with many fun activites to do. The famous thing about the Maldives is the stunning and spectacular resorts that are located on the beach with the white sand and crystal clear water.

France

Pantheon, Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Musee d'Orsay, adorable cafes, and Disneyland are all located in the magnificent Paris, France. When you travel to paris, you will never run out of anything to do! There is always something to do and to see.
